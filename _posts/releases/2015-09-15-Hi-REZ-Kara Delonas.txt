REZ comes together with Kara Delonas and really brings the vibe to the tropics!  Kara's huge vocals and clever lyrics blends beautifully on top of REZ's potent guitar licks, leaving the listener breathless by the end of every hook.  "Hi" will take you to another place.  An emotional roller coaster which resolves itself with mystical sounds and calming tones on the bridge.  A lot on the horizon for this powerful collaboration.
